Jan. 13 | Elise Hu

Why We’re Here and Social Media Reporting Tools

We’ll lay out the course objectives and do some “best of” show-and-tell, and we’ll spend the second half of the lab experimenting with using free social and blogging platforms for news.

Assignments 

  1. On Twitter, start following at least 50 new accounts that will comprise your feed. Start tweeting and talking with folks on the platform.
  2. Get started with Wordpress.com:
    • Set up your blog. This will be the URL for your running/final class project where you'll experiment and do your data and interactive assignments.
    • Add at least 2+ widgets. All your blogs will have RSS feeds, so you can use your own blog's feed to power some IFTTT recipes (See next assignment)
    • Write at least three posts between now and next class on the topic of your blog. Use appropriate links and images that you shoot, if you can. At least one post should embed a video or an image.
    • Build an "about" page that explains the purpose of your blog
    • Practice tagging and categorizing everything
    • Send your blog's URL to Elise at elisehu@gmail.com
  3. Sign up for IFTTT and use at least one recipe, or create your own.
  4. Skim The Data Journalism Handbook to be ready for next class.
